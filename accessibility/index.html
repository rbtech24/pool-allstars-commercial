Accessibility Statement

Our commitment to making this website accessible to everyone.

Last updated: May 2026

Our commitment

Pool All-Stars Commercial is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We continually improve the user experience for everyone and apply the relevant accessibility standards.

Conformance status

This website aims to conform with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA. WCAG defines requirements for designers and developers to improve accessibility for people with disabilities and includes the following:

  • Perceivable — information must be presentable to users in ways they can perceive (alt text on images, sufficient color contrast)
  • Operable — interface components must be operable (keyboard-accessible, no time-out traps)
  • Understandable — content and operation must be understandable (clear language, predictable navigation)
  • Robust — content must work with a range of assistive technologies (semantic HTML, ARIA where needed)

What we do

  • Semantic HTML structure throughout the site
  • Alt text on all meaningful images
  • Keyboard-accessible navigation including the mobile menu
  • Visible focus indicators on interactive elements
  • Color contrast ratios meeting WCAG AA targets
  • Responsive design that supports zoom up to 200%
  • Form labels paired with their inputs
  • ARIA landmarks and labels where helpful

Known limitations

Despite our efforts, some content may not be fully accessible. If you encounter an issue, please contact us so we can address it.

Feedback & contact

If you experience any accessibility barriers, or have suggestions for improvement, please contact us:

  • Phone: (833) 968-4888
  • Email: accessibility@poolallstars.com
  • Web form: Contact page

We aim to respond to accessibility feedback within 2 business days.

Assessment approach

We assess our website's accessibility through a combination of automated testing tools, manual review, and feedback from users.
