Frequently asked

What property managers ask us most.

What is included in commercial pool service?

Standard commercial weekly pool maintenance includes water chemistry testing and balancing (FAC, pH, alkalinity, calcium, CYA), brushing, vacuuming, basket emptying, equipment inspection, filter pressure check, and a same-day photo-confirmed service report.

How quickly can you provide a Certificate of Insurance?

COI is provided within 24 hours of contract signing, naming your property as additional insured. We carry general liability and workers' compensation insurance, and provide updated COIs annually.

Do you offer multi-property pricing?

Yes. Volume pricing kicks in at 3+ properties under one contract. Portfolios get a single dedicated account manager, consolidated monthly invoicing, and per-property cost allocation. Learn about multi-property pricing.

What areas of Florida do you serve?

We service commercial properties across Tampa Bay, Orlando, Jacksonville, Sarasota, Naples, South Florida, and the Space Coast — essentially statewide for commercial portfolios. See all service areas.

Are your technicians certified?

Yes. All technicians hold Certified Pool/Spa Operator (CPO) certification from the Pool & Hot Tub Alliance and complete OSHA chemical handling training. They are background-checked, uniformed, and identified by company badge on every visit.

What is your response time for repairs?

Standard repair response is same business day for diagnosis. Most repairs are completed within 48 hours. 24/7 emergency dispatch is available for contract customers experiencing pump failure, chemical issues, or guest-impacting problems.

How does your billing work?

Standard billing is net-30 monthly invoicing in advance. Multi-property portfolios receive consolidated invoices with per-property line items for accounting allocation. We support ACH, credit card, and traditional check payment.
